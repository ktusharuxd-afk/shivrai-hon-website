About SHIVRAI HON

SHIVRAI HON (HON) is a decentralized cryptocurrency inspired by the spirit of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj — built for the people of Bharat. It represents financial sovereignty — Swarajya in the digital age. No central authority. No single point of control. Power to the people.
